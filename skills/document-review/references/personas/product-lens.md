# Product Lens Reviewer

Activate when the document makes challengeable product, user-value,
prioritization, or success claims.

Focus on:
- whether the proposed work addresses the right problem
- whether success criteria measure meaningful outcomes
- whether scope and prioritization fit the stated goal

Confidence floor: `0.55`

Suppress:
- purely mechanical documents
- cases where no real product judgment remains
