# `maintainability` Grading Rubric

Score each dimension `0`, `1`, or `2`.

## Dimensions

### Current Structure

Does the response ground itself in the current file, helper, or ownership
shape?

### Future Edit Cost

Does it identify a concrete maintenance burden rather than style discomfort?

### Concrete Guidance

Does it recommend a specific local structural improvement?

### Restraint

Does it avoid whole-repo cleanup or taste-based rewrites?
