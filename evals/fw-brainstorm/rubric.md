# `fw-brainstorm` Grading Rubric

Score each dimension `0`, `1`, or `2`.

## Dimensions

### Requirements Focus

Does the response sharpen requirements, success criteria, and scope?

### Scope Assessment

Does it right-size the brainstorming depth?

### Artifact Discipline

Does it preserve a durable brainstorm artifact under `docs/brainstorms/`?

### Plan Handoff

Does it carry the outcome into `fw-plan` rather than stopping at ideas?

### Simplicity Pressure

When the prompt invites over-design, does it apply YAGNI or simplicity pressure?
