# `fw-incident` Grading Rubric

Score each dimension `0`, `1`, or `2`.

## Dimensions

### Evidence Grounding

Does it start from runtime evidence such as alerts, logs, traces, metrics, or
live symptoms?

### Blast Radius

Does it describe who or what is affected and how far the incident can spread?

### Decision Quality

Does it choose and justify mitigation, rollback, patch, or observe posture?

### Handoff Discipline

Does it route cleanly into debug, rollout, planning, work, or ship?

### Artifact Preservation

Does it preserve the evidence trail with a reusable incident or evidence-bundle
artifact?
